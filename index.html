Project Details

The Challenge:
Modern Systems, Historic Shell

Executed full MEP Revit modeling at LOD 350 for the seven-storey Battersea Power Station redevelopment. This massive undertaking covered approximately 50,000 m², requiring the seamless integration of mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and fire-protection systems into the strictly protected historic shell.

The team mapped complex ductwork, pipework, HVAC, and fire-sprinkler layouts against 2D service drawings and as-built surveys (Laser Scans). The critical task was resolving over 250 model clashes through intensive coordination workshops.

Outcome

Supplied coordinated shop drawings and clash-free BIM data in just 15 working days.

Scope

Full MEP Revit modeling & Clash Detection
